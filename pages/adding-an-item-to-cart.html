Adding an item to cart

Description

This section will guide the client through adding an item to their cart on the Louman Website.

Prerequisites

•        Client must be logged into the system

•        There must be enough stock on the system

Adding an item to cart process

Step 1: Select the Products option on the side bar.

Step 2: Click on the size or type filters to select size and type of product.

Step 3: Click on the add to cart button of the specific product to be added to the cart.

 

Step 4: Select the quantity of the product to be added to the cart.

 

Step 5: Click on the add button to confirm the process.

•        In order to successfully add an item to the cart there must be enough items in stock.
