Generate monthly sales report

Description

This section will guide the admin through generating a monthly sales report on the Louman Website.

Prerequisites

•        Admin must be logged into the system

Generate monthly sales report process

Step 1 Select the monthly sales report option on the side bar.

Step 2: Select a month from the calendar search bar and click the get report button on the monthly sales report screen.

Step 3: Click on the generate report button.

Step 4 : Click on the report download displayed at the bottom of the screen. The monthly sales report pdf will display.
