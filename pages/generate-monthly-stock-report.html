Generate monthly stock report

Description

This section will guide the admin through generating a monthly stock report on the Louman Website.

Prerequisites

•        Admin must be logged into the system

Generate monthly stock report process

Step 1 Select the monthly stock report option on the side bar.

Step 2: Select a month from the calendar search bar and click the get report button on the monthly stock report screen.

Step 3: Click on the generate report button.

Step 4 : Click on the report download displayed at the bottom of the screen. The monthly stock report pdf will display.

Exercise

•        Click the calendar search bar and select a month to generate a report for.

•        Click the get report button

•        Click the generate report button.

•        Open the downloaded report on the bottom left of the screen to open the pdf.

Summary

You have now learnt how to download a monthly stock report successfully.
