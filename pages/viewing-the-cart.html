Viewing the cart

Description

This section will guide the client through viewing their cart on the Louman Website.

Prerequisites

•        Client must be logged into the system

Viewing the cart process

Step 1: Select the products option on the side bar.

Step 2: Select the go to cart button.

 

 

Exercise

•        Click on the Products sidebar.

•        Click on the go to cart button.

Summary

You have now learnt how to view the order cart on the Louman system.

•        For the cart screen to display anything there needs to be an item in the client’s cart.
