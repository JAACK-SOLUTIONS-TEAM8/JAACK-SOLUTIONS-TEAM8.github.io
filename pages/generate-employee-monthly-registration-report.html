Generate employee monthly registration report

Description

This section will guide the admin through generating an employee monthly registration report on the Louman Website.

Prerequisites

•        Admin must be logged into the system

Generate employee monthly registration report process

Step 1 Select the monthly registration report option on the side bar.

Step 2: Select a month from the calendar search bar and click the get report button on the employee monthly registration report screen.

 

Step 3: Click on the download report button.

Step 4 : Click on the report download displayed at the bottom of the screen. The monthly registration report pdf will display.

Exercise

•        Click the calendar search bar and select a month to generate a report for.

•        Click the get report button

•        Click the generate report button.

•        Open the downloaded report on the bottom left of the screen to open the pdf.

Summary

You have now learnt how to download a monthly registration report successfully.
