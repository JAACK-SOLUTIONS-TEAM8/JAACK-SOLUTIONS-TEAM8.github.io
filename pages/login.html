Login

Description

This section will guide the User through logging in on the Louman Website.

Prerequisites

Searching of a Client process

Step 1: Select the Login option on the Nav bar.

Step 2: Click on the Username textbox and the password textbox on the Login screen.

Step 3: Input the Login criteria.

Step 4: Click on the Login button to proceed with the Login.

Step 5 : Enter the confirmation code

Step 6: Click on the confirm button
