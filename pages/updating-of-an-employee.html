Updating of an employee

Description

This section will guide the admin through updating an existing employees’ details on the Louman Website.

Prerequisites

•        Admin must be logged into the system

•        Employee must exist on the system

Updating employee details process

Step 1: Select the Employee details option on the side bar.

Step 2: Select the view button in the Employee detail table on the Employee detail screen.

Step 3: Change the relevant details on the view employee detail screen.

Step 4 : Click on the Update button to proceed with the employee update or the Cancel button to stop the process.

Step 5: Click on the button on the confirmation notification to confirm the update or the cancel button to return to the view employee detail screen.
