Checkout

Description

This section will guide the client through checking out and placing an order on the Louman Website.

Prerequisites

•        Client must be logged into the system

•        The client must have an item in their cart

Checkout process

•        Step 1: Select the products option on the side bar.

•        Step 2: Select the go to cart button.

 

Step 3: Click on the proceed button to checkout.

 

 

Step 4: Select a delivery type from the dropdown and complete the required order information .

Step 5: Click on the checkout button.

Step 6: Click on the yes button on the confirmation notification to confirm the checkout or the cancel button to return to checkout screen.

Exercise

•        Click the go to cart button on the Products screen.

•        Click on the proceed button on the cart screen.

•        Type in the checkout details:

Field

Test Data

Delivery

Pickup

Payment

Cash

 

•        Click on the checkout button.

Summary

You have now learnt how to check out on the Louman system to place an order.
