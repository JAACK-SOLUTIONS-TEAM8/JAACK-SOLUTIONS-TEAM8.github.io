Removing item from cart

Description

This section will guide the client through removing an item from their cart on the Louman Website.

Prerequisites

•        Client must be logged into the system

•        The product must be added to the client’s cart

Removing item from cart process

Step 1: Select the products option on the side bar.

Step 2: Select the go to cart button.

Step 3: Click on the remove from cart button underneath the item the client wants to remove.

Step 4: Click on the yes button on the confirmation notification to confirm the update or the cancel button to return to the view team screen.

 

 

 

 

 

 

Exercise

•        Click on the go to cart button the product screen.

•        Click the remove from cart button under the product to be removed.

•        Click the remove button to remove the item.

•        Click the Cancel button to return to the Products screen.

Summary

You have now learnt how to remove an item from the cart successfully.
