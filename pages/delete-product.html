Delete Product

 

Description: This section will guide the admin through the steps of deleting a product.

Prerequisites: The admin must be logged in

Steps:

•        Step 1: The admin clicks on the “Products” option on the features sidebar.

•        Step 2: The products screen is displayed, and the admin will then press on the delete button

•        Step 3: The admin will then confirm deleting the product.

 

 

 

 

Exercise

•        Click the Delete button on the Products screen and delete the apple product.

 

Summary

You have now learnt how to delete a product successfully with its corresponding details.

•        To successfully delete a product, the product must exist in the database.
